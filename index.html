Friendship Nepal Tours and Travels

Friendship Nepal Tours and Travels is a fully licensed and registered travel agency / company in Nepal, recognized by the Government of Nepal’s tourism authority, Nepal Association of Tour & Travel Agents (NATTA), and Nepal Tourism Board (NTB). We specialize in providing comprehensive travel packages for leisure travelers, groups, charter tours, and business professionals, supported by a real-time website and an unmatched selection of products.

Our Journey and Commitment

Since our establishment in 2003, we have rapidly grown into one of Nepal’s leading travel companies.
